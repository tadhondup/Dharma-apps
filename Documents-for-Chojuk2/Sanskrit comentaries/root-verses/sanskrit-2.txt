\c 2
\mt1 Chapter-2 Purifying bad deeds
\b
\v 5 taccittaratnagrahaṇāya samyakpūjāṃ karomy eṣa tathāgatānām |
saddharmaratnasya ca nirmalasya buddhātmajānāṃ ca guṇodadhīnām
\b
\v 6 yāvanti puṣpāṇi phalāni caiva bhaiṣajyajātāni ca yāni santi |
ratnāni yāvanti ca santi loke jalāni ca svacchamanoramāṇi
\b
\v 7 mahīdharā ratnamayās tathānye vanapradeśāś ca vivekaramyāḥ |
latāḥ supuṣpābharaṇojjvalāś ca drumāś ca ye satphalanamraśākhāḥ
\b
\v 8 devādilokeṣu ca gandhadhūpāḥ kalpadrumā ratnamayāś ca vṛkṣāḥ |
sarāṃsi cāmbhoruhabhūṣaṇāni haṃsasvanātyantamanoharāṇi
